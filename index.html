5 Dietary tips to sustain a healthy lifestyle.

  • 1. Do not skip breakfast

    • • Consume breakfast to avoid overly snacking throughout the day

    • Eating breakfast will help kick start your metabolism

  • 2. Stay Hydrated

    • • Drink 2 liters of water everyday

    •Limit your caffeine to 100mg per day

    •Try to incorporate a detox drink once a while

  • 3. Incorporate more vegetables and fruits into your diet

    • • 2/3 of your plate should be filled with vegetables and fruits

    • Try to have vegetables with every meal

  • 4. Limit sugar intake

    • • Stick to natural sugars; ex. honey, fruits

    • Substitute your sugary drinks with honey

    • Avoid soda drinks, sweets with high sugar

  • 5. Read nutrient labels

    • • Completely avoid food containing trans fat

    • Avoid high calorie food

    • Look for food containing whole ingredients

DIY Skin Care
  • For oily skin:

    • • Use oil free cleansers on a daily basis

    • Use oil free moisturizers and other skin care

    • Apply clay mask to help absorb oils and control shine

  • For dry skin:

    • • Use hydrating skin cleansers that does not strip oils from skin

    • Looks for moisturizers that contain ceramides, glycerin, or hyaluronic acid

DIY Nail Care:
  • Apply cuticle oil for healthier looking nails

  • Mix olive oil and lemon as a treatment to help grow your nails

  • Try to limit acrylic nail application to a minimum

“Health is a state of body. Wellness is a state of mind.”- J. Stanford

Health starts from the inside out. Maintaining a healthy mind is a key to maintaining a healthy body. It is important to focus on your mental health, to better your thoughts, relations, and productivity.

  • Try to get 8 hours of quality sleep everyday

  • Try to surround yourself with people that inspire you

  • Exercise 3-4 days a week

  • Get fresh air

  • Read for enjoyment

  • Spend quality time with friends and family

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Should I avoid carbs?

    No. Carbs is a part of a whole diet and you need carbs to help provide you with energy.

  • Should I avoid sugar as a whole?

    No. Food containing natural sugar should not be avoided because they can provide you with essential nutrients.

  • How many servings of fruits and vegetables do we need per day?

    You need 2 servings of fruits and 3 servings of vegetables per day.

  • How does yoga improve health?

    Yoga helps clear the mind and lowers stress levels.

  • How often should I trim my hair?

    It is recommended to trim your hair every 6 weeks to promote healthy hair growth.
